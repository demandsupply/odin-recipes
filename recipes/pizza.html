Pizza's Recipe

Description

This easy pizza dough recipe is great for beginners and produces a soft homemade pizza crust. Skip the pizza delivery because you only need few basic ingredients to begin!

Ingredients

  • 280 g all-purpose flour
  • 7 g dried yeast
  • 150 ml lukewarm water
  • 4 tsp sugar
  • 20 ml olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ½ onion
  • 250 ml puréed tomatoes (canned)
  • 125 g Buffalo mozzarella
  • 20 g arugula leaves
  • 100 g Parma ham
  • 3 figs salt pepper

Steps

  1. For the dough, first mix flour, sugar, and salt. Dissolve the yeast in lukewarm water and roughly knead with the flour mixture.
  2. Now add olive oil and work mixture into a smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough with a kitchen towel and allow to rise in a warm place for approx. 1 - 2 hours, until it has doubled in size.
  4. For the sauce, finely dice garlic and onion.
  5. Next, sauté the onion and garlic in vegetable oil until translucent. Pour in the pureed tomatoes and season with sugar and a pinch of salt and pepper. Leave the sauce to reduce for approx. 5 – 7 min. on a low heat.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C/ 355°F. Next, roll out the dough to be approx. 0.5 cm thin and place on a lined baking tray.
  7. Spread the tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  8. Now, cut the mozzarella into slices and lay them evenly across the pizza. Then, bake the pizza in a preheated oven at 180°C/355°F for approx. 20 - 25 min. until golden brown.
  9. As soon as the pizza is finished, cover with arugula leaves and Parma ham. Quarter figs and distribute over the pizza . Enjoy served warm.
